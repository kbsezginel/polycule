About this preview
Brightness is computed by a 1:1 JavaScript port of the firmware's renderStep() / renderContinuous() and animUnitMs() (free-run, one unit = one beat at the set BPM; tail fade b -= b>>2). The amber glow adds a small filament thermal lag so the bulbs behave like real incandescents rather than snapping instantly — toggle it off to see the raw values. Shortcuts: Space play/pause · R re-sync · 1–8 pick animation. To capture a GIF, screen-record the stage (e.g. with your OS recorder or ffmpeg).
