# One-shot
One-shot audio playback and MP3 player device

## DIY

### What you need
- Arduino Uno
- DFPlayer mini ()
- LEDs (I use a 8 LED Neopixel strip)
- 4x4 Keypad
- One 3-way toggle switch
- 2 10K Potentiometers
